MOONSHINE BY ARTHUR HOPKINS

LUKE.

It hain't no luck to drink alone when you git company. Better have some.

REVENUE.

Very well, my friend, I suffer willingly.

[_Drinks a little and chokes._

LUKE.

[_Draining cup._]

I reckon ye all don't like the flavor of liquor that hain't been stamped.
